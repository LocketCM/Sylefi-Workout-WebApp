# Sylefi Wellness — Client Guide

Welcome to Sylefi Wellness! This short guide walks you through getting signed in and using the app.

---

## Step 1 — Joining for the first time

Meg will send you a **join link** and a **6-character code** (something like `ABC123`). The link looks like this:

`https://locketcm.github.io/Sylefi-Workout-WebApp/#/join?code=ABC123`

1. **Tap the link** on your phone (or open it in any browser)
2. The code is filled in for you automatically — if it's not, type it in (uppercase letters and numbers)
3. Tap **Verify Code**
4. Confirm your email and phone number (these are optional, but helpful so Meg can reach you)
5. Tap **Join**
6. Done! You'll be taken right to your dashboard.

> **Tip:** After you've joined once, you stay signed in. You won't need the code again.

> Your invite code is good for **7 days**. If it expires before you use it, just ask Meg for a new one.

---

## Your personal sign-in link

After you join, your coach can give you a **permanent sign-in link** that looks like:

`https://locketcm.github.io/Sylefi-Workout-WebApp/#/signin?code=AB12CD34EF56`

This is different from the one-time invite code — it's your personal link that **never expires** and works on any device. **Bookmark it.** If you ever get signed out (new phone, cleared browser, etc.), you can always use this link to get straight back to your dashboard.

Treat it like a password — anyone who has it can sign in as you.

---

## Step 2 — Add the app to your phone (optional but recommended)

This makes Sylefi feel like a real app instead of a website.

**iPhone (Safari):**
1. Open the app in Safari
2. Tap the **Share** button (square with arrow)
3. Scroll down and tap **Add to Home Screen**
4. Tap **Add**

**Android (Chrome):**
1. Open the app in Chrome
2. Tap the **three-dot menu** in the top-right
3. Tap **Add to Home screen** (or **Install app**)
4. Tap **Add**

Now Sylefi is one tap away from your home screen.

---

## Your Dashboard

When you open the app, you'll see one of two things:

### "Your program is ready"
You have a published program from Meg. You'll see:
- **Program title** (e.g., "Week of April 7")
- **Progress** ("2 of 4 workouts complete")
- A **list of workouts** — tap any one to start

### "Your program is not yet ready"
Meg is still working on your program. As soon as she publishes it, this screen updates **instantly** — you don't need to refresh.

---

## Doing a workout

1. Tap a workout from your dashboard
2. You'll see a list of exercises with the target sets, reps, and weight (e.g., "Squats — 3 × 10 @ 95 lb")
3. For each exercise:
   - Log your **actual** sets, reps, and weight as you go
   - Tap the **checkbox** when you finish that exercise — it turns green with a little buzz
4. The progress bar at the top fills as you go
5. There's a **notes box** at the bottom of each workout — leave Meg a note about how it felt, anything that hurt, etc.
6. When all exercises are checked off, tap **Finish Workout**
7. Confetti! Workout saved.

> **You can stop anytime.** Your progress saves automatically. If you close the app and come back later, you'll pick up exactly where you left off — the workout will show "In progress" on your dashboard.

---

## Watching exercise videos

If Meg added a video link for an exercise, you'll see a **video icon** next to it. Tap to watch — handy if you're not sure how to do a movement.

---

## Messaging Meg

Tap the **chat bubble** at the top of your dashboard, or the **Contact Coach** card.

- Type your message and tap Send (or press Enter)
- Meg's replies show up instantly — no refresh needed
- If you have unread messages, you'll see a **badge** on the chat icon and the **Contact Coach card turns teal**

---

## Workout history

Tap the **history icon** at the top of your dashboard to see every workout you've logged. Tap any workout to see the details — what you actually lifted, your notes, and Meg's notes.

This is great for tracking your progress over time.

---

## Dark mode

Tap the **sun/moon icon** at the top to switch between light and dark mode. The app remembers your preference.

---

## Signing out

Tap the **person icon** at the top of your dashboard. You usually don't need to do this — the app keeps you signed in. Only sign out if you're on a shared device.

If you sign out, you'll need Meg to send you a new join link to get back in.

---

## Troubleshooting

**"That code doesn't match any invite, or it has expired."**
Either the code was typed wrong, the 7 days are up, or it was already used. Ask Meg for a fresh code.

**The app shows a white screen / won't load.**
Close the browser tab and reopen the app. If it still doesn't work, try a different browser or text Meg.

**I got signed out and the invite code doesn't work anymore.**
Your one-time invite code only works once. Use your **personal sign-in link** instead (the long one with `signin?code=` in the URL) — that one never expires. If you don't have it, ask Meg to send it to you.

**My workout progress disappeared.**
It shouldn't — the app saves automatically every few seconds. If something looks wrong, screenshot it and send it to Meg.

**I accidentally checked off an exercise I didn't do.**
Just tap the checkbox again to uncheck it. Nothing is locked in until you tap **Finish Workout**, and even then Meg can see your actual logged numbers.
